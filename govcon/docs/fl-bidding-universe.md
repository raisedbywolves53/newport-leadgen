# Florida Bidding Universe — Validated Feb 28, 2026

## Channel 1: Federal Direct

### Micro-Purchase (<$15K)
- **Opps/year in FL:** 1,500-2,000
- **Avg contract value:** $7,500 (midpoint $3K-$12K)
- **Avg bidders:** 1.0-1.2 (most are sole source / P-card)
- **Sole source %:** 93% for NAICS 424490 @ DoD (FPDS FY2024, HIGH confidence)
- **Invisible on SAM.gov** — requires GovSpend to see
- **Confidence:** MEDIUM (83% micro-purchase ratio extrapolation from 2,421 national awards)

### Simplified Acquisition ($15K-$350K)
- **Opps/year in FL:** 350-500
- **Avg contract value:** $85,000 (midpoint $50K-$150K)
- **Avg bidders:** 1.6-3.2 (varies: 1.6 for confectionery, 2.4 for DoD 424410, 3.2 for BOP 424490)
- **Confidence:** HIGH (FPDS FY2024 validated)

### Large (>$350K)
- **Opps/year in FL:** 50-70
- **Avg contract value:** $750,000 (midpoint $500K-$1M for Newport's Year 4-5 targets)
- **Avg bidders:** 3-5+

- **BuyBoard** — win one contract, all 67 FL districts can purchase without additional bidding

## Channel 5: Subcontracting

### DLA Prime Vendor Subs
- DLA runs 55 prime vendor contracts, $3.79B total
- FL bases served by East CONUS prime vendor (likely Sysco or US Foods)
- Primes MUST maintain SB subcontracting plans (FAR 52.219-9) on contracts >$750K
- Typical sub value: $250K-$1M/yr per relationship
- 3-6 relationships possible

### FSMC Subs (Aramark, Compass, Sodexo)
- These companies manage food at FL DOC, universities, hospitals
- They need food suppliers — Newport can be a supply sub
- Typical sub value: $500K-$5M/yr per FSMC
- 3-5 relationships possible

### USDA Commodity Distribution
- TEFAP, LFPA, Farm to School via FDACS
- LFPA explicitly designed for local/regional distributors
- 3-5 bids/yr, $200K-$2M each
- FDACS administers — Newport should register as FDACS vendor

### Mentor-Protege (Year 2+)
- SBA and DoD programs available
- Newport is ideal protege: 35yr FL ops, warehouse/fleet, W-2 workforce, zero federal past performance (target profile)
- JV with mentor can bid on SB set-asides
- $1M-$5M/yr potential (Year 3+)
