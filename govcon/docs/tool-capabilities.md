# Tool Capabilities & Costs — Validated Feb 28, 2026

## Paid Route Stack

### CLEATUS ($3,360/yr — $280/mo annual billing)
- **What:** All-in-one: discovery (40K+ sources, federal + SLED) + AI proposal writing + bid/no-bid PWin scoring + CRM
- **Throughput:** Case studies show 3-6x proposal speed. Real users: 10-12 proposals/month (up from 2-3 manual)
- **Discovery:** Scans 40K+ public sector sources. Translates NAICS to NIGP for SLED. Daily AI-matched alerts.
- **Contract types:** Full spectrum (RFP, RFQ, IFB, combined synopsis)
- **Unknowns:** "AI credits" system — unclear if there's a hard cap at high volume (15+ proposals/month). Need sales confirmation.
- **Sources:** cleat.ai/pricing, cleat.ai/case-studies

### HigherGov ($500/yr Starter, $2,500 Standard)
- **What:** Market intel + opportunity discovery. 4M+ federal opps, 1M+ SLED opps, 1M+ DLA opps, 61M+ award records.
- **SLED coverage:** 40,000+ agencies, all 50 states. Adds NAICS classification to SLED opps (SLED doesn't natively use NAICS).
- **Alerts:** Unlimited saved searches, real-time/daily/weekly email. Updated every 20 minutes.
- **FL food/grocery estimate:** 15-40 biddable opps/month (federal + FL SLED combined). Seasonal peaks March-June.
- **Limits:** Unlimited searches/alerts all tiers. Export: 1,000 records/search (Starter).
- **Sources:** highergov.com/pricing, github.com/HigherGov/API

### GovSpend ($7,000-$12,000/yr — negotiated, avg ~$10K)
- **What:** SLED purchase order intelligence. 1.08B+ purchase orders, 21K-30K agencies.
- **How:** Tens of thousands of FOIA requests monthly to agencies for actual PO data.
- **Unique value:** Shows the 80%+ of govt purchasing that happens WITHOUT an RFP — P-card transactions, routine POs, recurring orders.
- **For Newport:** FL school district cafeteria purchases, county jail food, municipal programs — all the small-dollar, high-frequency transactions.
- **Contacts:** Procurement officer directory with phone/email for direct outreach.
- **Data quality:** 5/10 independent rating — contacts can be outdated, PO data has gaps. UI rated 9.5/10.
- **Risks:** Rigid contracts with auto-renewal. Negotiate hard, remove auto-renewal.
- **Sources:** govspend.com, vendr.com/marketplace/gov-spend

## Free Route (SAM.gov only — $0)
- **What it shows:** Federal opportunities above $25,000 only. ~24K new notices/month nationally, ~7,600 open at any time.
- **FL food NAICS visible:** ~10-15 opportunities/month
- **What it misses:** All micro-purchases (<$15K) = 83.4% of food contracts. ALL SLED = $0. Pre-solicitation intel = $0. P-card spend = $0.
- **Free alerts:** Yes — saved search email notifications (daily/weekly), but minimal detail.
