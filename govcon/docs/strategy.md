### Where They're Beatable

1. **Small contracts they won't bother with.** Sysco/US Foods ($50B+ rev) will not chase a $200K school district supply contract in Polk County
2. **Local preference programs.** FL counties give 5-10% price preference to in-state vendors
3. **Small business set-asides.** ~23% of federal contract dollars set aside; Sysco/US Foods/Aramark legally excluded
4. **Agencies burned by big-vendor quality failures.** Aramark: maggots in food (MI), meal-cutting lawsuits (Dec 2025). Trinity: OK voided $74M contract after 4 weeks (2025). Agencies actively seeking alternatives
5. **States that dropped FSMCs.** MI terminated Aramark 2018. CT, MA moved corrections food in-house. Need new suppliers
6. **Cooperative purchasing.** Once on a coop's approved list, dozens of agencies can buy with a phone call

### Where NOT to Compete

1. DLA Subsistence Prime Vendor contracts ($100M+ infrastructure required)
2. National FSMC management contracts (Aramark/Trinity/Compass territory)
3. Agencies with 10+ year incumbent on auto-renewal
4. Federal agencies requiring nationwide delivery

### 4-Phase Entry Timeline

**Phase 1: Foundation (Months 1-3) -- FL Local, Lowest Barriers**

Target: FL school districts, county facilities, state agencies.
- Register on MFMP, DemandStar, county portals
- Introduce Newport to 5-10 mid-size FL school districts (Polk, Seminole, Volusia, Brevard, Lee, Manatee, Sarasota)
- Respond to every food supply RFQ/IFB from FL school districts and counties
- Target micro-purchase opportunities ($0-$10K) at county jails and gov cafeterias
- Attend FSNA conference
- Create capability statement and legitimacy package

Expected results: 3-5 small contracts ($10K-$100K), initial past performance references.

**Phase 2: Federal + State Vendor Channels (Months 3-6)**

Target: FL BOP institutions, FL military bases (via prime vendor), USDA AMS, FL DOC (via Aramark).
- Complete SAM.gov registration
- Apply to USDA AMS Qualified Bidders List
- Contact FL BOP food service administrators (Coleman, Marianna, Miami, Tallahassee) with product samples
- Contact Sysco Gov / US Foods about local sub-vendor for FL military bases
- Contact Aramark Corrections FL region about approved supplier status
- Apply to Foodbuy GPO
- Join ACFSA ($150/yr), SNA/FSNA ($75/yr)

Expected results: USDA AMS approved status, first federal micro-purchases, approved supplier to 1+ prime vendor/FSMC.

**Phase 3: SE Regional Expansion (Months 6-12)**

Target: GA, AL, SC, NC state portals. FSMC vendor applications. GPO membership. Larger federal bids.
- Register on 5 SE state procurement portals
- Submit vendor applications to Aramark, Compass, Sodexo, Trinity, Summit
- Bid on larger FL school district contracts ($250K+)
- Attend ACFSA annual conference (August) and ALA conference (January)
- Join ALA ($500/yr)

Expected results: Active contracts in 3+ FL school districts, approved vendor for 1-2 FSMCs, initial SE regional contracts.

**Phase 4: Scale (Month 12+)**

Target: National federal contracts, multi-state school nutrition cooperatives, GSA Schedule.
- Bid on multi-facility BOP food supply proposals
- Pursue GSA MAS listing
- Evaluate cooperative purchasing vehicle performance
- Build CPARS past performance record
- Dedicated government sales FTE decision
- Year 2 target: $2M-$5M government revenue
