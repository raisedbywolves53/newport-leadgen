## Section 3: Fulfillment Overhead

### The 5% Figure Is Defensible — But Varies by Tier

#### Bottom-Up Component Analysis

| Component | % of Gov Revenue | What It Covers |
|---|---|---|
| Documentation & compliance reporting | 1.5-3.0% | Buy American tracking, delivery receipts, WAWF invoicing, district reporting, temperature logs |
| Product substitution restrictions | 0.5-1.0% | Higher safety stock for gov-allocated inventory, emergency sourcing |
| Insurance differential | 0.4-1.6% (Y1) / negligible (Y5) | Additional insured riders, umbrella policy increases |
| Delivery scheduling constraints | 0.5-1.0% | Security checkpoint time at bases/jails, school delivery windows |
| Quality assurance / inspection | 0.25-0.5% | USDA specs, product samples, receiving inspection |
| Contract administration | 0.5-1.0% | Portal management, modifications, audit responses, CPARS |
| **TOTAL** | **3.65-8.1%** | |
| **Weighted midpoint** | **~4.5-5.0%** | |

**Verdict: 5% is defensible as a conservative blended figure (MEDIUM-HIGH confidence).**

#### Per-Tier Overhead Rates

| Tier | Overhead | Rationale |
|---|---|---|
| Federal micro (<$15K) | 1.5% | Near-commercial, minimal docs, no WAWF |
| Federal simplified ($15K-$350K) | 4.0% | WAWF, Buy American docs, insurance |
| SLED school districts | 5.0% | District portals, strict substitution, temp logs, USDA reporting |
| SLED corrections | 6.0% | Security delays, HACCP, stricter protocols |
| Set-aside | 4.0% | Similar to simplified |
| DLA sub | 2.5% | Prime handles most compliance |

### Critical DSO Correction

**Federal food payment is FASTER than the model assumes.**

Per FAR 52.232-25 (Prompt Payment Act):
| Product | Payment Due |
|---|---|
| Meat, poultry, fish, eggs | **7 days** after delivery |
| Perishable agricultural commodities | **10 days** after delivery |
| Dairy, edible fats/oils | **10 days** after invoice receipt |
| All other goods/services | **30 days** after invoice receipt |

SLED: Net 30-45 typical for school districts and counties.

**Blended DSO for Newport's portfolio: 20-30 days (not 45).** Federal food at 7-15 days pulls the average down significantly.
