## 4. Buyer-by-Buyer Assessment

### DOJ/Bureau of Prisons — #1 FL Small Contract Buyer

- **FL value:** $3.7M / 71 contracts (live USASpending data). **Confidence: HIGH.**
- **National food budget:** ~$401M/year, 479K meals/day, 122 institutions.
- **FL institutions:** FCC Coleman (7,100 inmates, largest complex nationally), FCI Miami, FDC Miami, FCI Marianna, FCI Tallahassee, FPC Pensacola.
- **Procurement:** Quarterly subsistence solicitations per institution on SAM.gov. Split by category (dry goods, meat, dairy, produce, bakery). Firm fixed price, FOB destination. Small business set-aside.
- **FCC Coleman alone:** ~$6-10M/year in food. Quarterly solicitations = 16+ bids/year.
- **FL BOP total:** ~$10-16M/year across all institutions.
- **Key competitor:** Rainmaker Inc ($5.1M in FL DOJ contracts).
- **Newport entry:** SAM.gov registration + quarterly subsistence bids. Start at FCI Miami or FPC Pensacola (smaller, less competition), then scale to Coleman.

### DoD — #2 FL Food Buyer

- **FL value:** $2.3M / 43 contracts (live data). **Confidence: HIGH.**
- **93% sole source for NAICS 424490 @ DoD** — confirmed by FPDS. Average 1.2 offers per award.
- **Mechanism:** Local base-level purchasing via GPC or simplified acquisition. DLA Subsistence Prime Vendor handles most volume, but individual installations buy supplemental/perishable items locally.
- **FL bases with food buying:** MacDill AFB (Tampa), Homestead ARB, NAS Jacksonville, Patrick SFB, NAS Pensacola, Eglin AFB.
- **Key insight:** Almost no competition. Base contracting officers need local vendors with cold chain.
- **Also:** Defense Commissary Agency (DeCA) operates 236 commissaries — parallel grocery channel via DeCA Form 40-15.

### FEMA — Narrow, Concentrated

- **Live data:** 11 contracts, $69.3M (FY2024-25). **Confidence: HIGH.**
- **2 vendors hold 97% of spending.** Alabama Dept of Rehab Services ($23.3M, CDP dining). Pistol Point Logistics ($22.7M, Hurricane Helene food bank delivery).
- **Direct FEMA food procurement is MRE-dominated** — $28.1M composite food packages, $8.5M special dietary.
- **Newport's FEMA play is NOT direct contracts.** Instead: (1) Register in FEMA Disaster Response Registry, (2) register as FL DEM vendor (850-815-4606), (3) position for micro-purchases during declared disasters ($25K threshold), (4) subcontract to FEMA primes during FL hurricane response. FL is #1 state for federal emergency declarations.
- **Revenue estimate:** $0 in no-hurricane year, $500K-5M in active season. Average over 3 years: $300K-1M/year.

### FL Department of Corrections

- **FL DOC:** ~80,000 inmates, estimated $50-100M/year food spend. **Confidence: LOW** — SLED data not in federal systems.
- **Model:** Contracted out (Aramark historically). Newport's path = sell to the FSMC contractor, not the DOC directly.
- **State procurement portal:** MyFloridaMarketPlace (MFMP).

### FL School Districts

- **67 districts, 2.8M students, estimated $500M-$1B annual food purchases.** **Confidence: LOW** (industry estimate).
- **NSLP nationally:** $17.7B, 4.8B lunches FY2024.
- **Top FL districts by food spend:** Miami-Dade ($80-120M), Broward ($60-90M), Hillsborough ($50-80M), Orange ($45-70M), Palm Beach ($40-65M).
- **Procurement:** Decentralized ITBs per district. Lowest responsive bidder wins (FL Statute Ch. 287). Most align with July-June fiscal year, ITBs issued Feb-May.
- **Realistic entry:** Category-specific ITBs at mid-size districts (Polk, Lee, Brevard). Year 1 target: 1-2 wins, $2-8M.

### VA Medical Centers

- **National SPV contract held by US Foods ($263M/yr).** Near-impenetrable barrier.
- **Exceptions only:** Fresh bread, milk, produce excluded from SPV. Local vendors can compete for these.
- **VISN 8 FL facilities:** James A. Haley (Tampa), Bay Pines, Miami VA, Orlando VA, West Palm Beach VA, Gainesville VA.
- **Realistic value:** $0-200K/year. Not a primary target.

## 6. Data Source Reference

### SAM.gov — Contract Opportunities + Entity Registration

- **URL:** https://sam.gov
- **Auth:** API key required. Free registration, 10 req/day public, 1,000/day registered.
- **Rate limit:** 1,000 requests/day with API key.
- **Key endpoints:**
  - `GET /opportunities` — active solicitations (pre-sol, solicitation, sources sought, etc.)
  - Entity search — registered vendors by NAICS/state/size
- **Key params:** NAICS codes (424410, 424490, etc.), PSC codes (89xx), place of performance state, posting date range, ptype (solicitation type).
- **Limitations:** Only contracts above micro-purchase threshold ($15K). Does not cover state/local.
- **Our client:** `enrichment/sam_client.py` (opportunities), `enrichment/sam_entity_client.py` (entity registry).
- **Rate limit management:** 24hr cache in `data/cache/`.

### USASpending.gov — Federal Spending Tracking

- **URL:** https://api.usaspending.gov/
- **Auth:** None required.
- **Rate limit:** No formal limit, but throttling at high volumes.
- **Key endpoints:**
  - `/api/v2/search/spending_by_category/naics/` — aggregate spending by NAICS
  - `/api/v2/search/spending_by_award/` — individual award details
  - `/api/v2/search/spending_by_geography/` — geographic breakdowns
  - `/api/v2/recipient/` — recipient/vendor profiles
  - `/api/v2/search/new_awards_over_time/` — trend analysis
  - `/api/v2/bulk_download/` — bulk download with filters
- **Key filters:** NAICS, agency, recipient, location, date range, award amount range.
- **Our client:** `enrichment/usaspending_client.py` (enhanced: awards, spending_by_recipient, spending_by_county, spending_by_geography, recipient_profile, new_awards_over_time, search_subawards).
- **Confirmed working:** market-size, small-contracts, fema, spending_by_recipient, spending_by_geography, new_awards_over_time — all validated Feb 2026.

### FPDS — Federal Procurement Data System

- **URL:** https://www.fpds.gov
- **Auth:** None required. Free to search.
- **Rate limit:** No formal limit.
- **Access:** Web search interface + ATOM feed API + data.gov API (`https://catalog.data.gov/dataset/federal-procurement-data-system-fpds-api`).
- **Coverage:** All federal contracts over $25,000.
- **Key search params:** NAICS code, PSC code, agency, vendor name, dollar amount, date range, competition type (sole source, competitive, etc.).
- **Our client:** `enrichment/fpds_client.py` — competition density analysis.
- **Confirmed working:** 111 records for NAICS 424410 in 2-month window. 537 total awards across 32 NAICS/agency combos validated.

### Grants.gov — Federal Grants

- **URL:** https://www.grants.gov
- **Auth:** None required.
- **Key endpoints:** `/grantsws/rest/opportunities/search` — search grants by keyword, agency, eligibility.
- **Key filters:** USDA food-related grants, CFDA numbers for food programs.
- **Our client:** `enrichment/grants_client.py`.
- **Confirmed working:** 136 food-related results validated Feb 2026.
