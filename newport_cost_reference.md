## SECTION 1: Government Contracting Costs

### Mandatory — Do These Now ($0 in fees, ~40 hours internal time)

| Item | Fee | Recurring | Time | Notes |
|------|----:|----------:|------|-------|
| SAM.gov registration + UEI | $0 | $0 (renew annually) | 2-10 business days | Free per SAM.gov. Set calendar reminder for annual renewal. |
| NAICS selection (424410, 424450, 424490) | $0 | $0 | 1-2 weeks | Write a 1-page rationale memo. Primary = preponderance of receipts. |
| Federal opportunity monitoring (SAM Contract Opportunities) | $0 | $0 | Same day | Set up saved searches for grocery/food supply solicitations. |
| Florida Substitute W-9 (DFS portal) | $0 | $0 | Days for IRS verification | Required for any FL state payments. |
| Records retention SOP (FAR 4.7 / FAR 4.703) | $0 | $0 | 1-2 weeks | 3-year retention after final payment (baseline). Template this once. |
| FAR 52.204-21 basic safeguarding checklist | $0 | $0 | 1-2 weeks | 15 basic controls for federal contract information. Document compliance. |

### Near-Term — Activate Within 90 Days ($0-$500 in fees)

| Item | Fee | Recurring | Time | Notes |
|------|----:|----------:|------|-------|
| MFMP vendor portal + VBS registration | $0 | **1% transaction fee** on eligible FL payments | 2-6 weeks | Factor the 1% into pricing/margins on state contracts. |
| City of Plantation vendor application | $0 | $0 (annual re-application) | 1-2 weeks | Set annual calendar reminder. Solicitations >$35K via DemandStar. |
| Broward County BPRO registration | $0 | $0 | 1-2 weeks | Required to respond to Broward solicitations. |

### Practically Required — Insurance ($265-$3,030/yr)

| Coverage | Annual Low | Annual Typical | Annual High | Notes |
|----------|----------:|---------------:|------------:|-------|
| General liability | $265 | $810 | $3,030 | Newport likely already carries this as a wholesaler. Verify COI requirements match municipal needs. |
| Product liability | Bundled | Bundled | Bundled | Usually bundled with GL for food/grocery wholesalers. Confirm with broker. |
| Cyber liability | $500 | $1,740 | $7,500 | Only if handling sensitive data. Unlikely for commercial food supply. Skip initially. |

### Build Phase — Professional Help ($1,500-$10,000 one-time)

| Item | One-Time Low | One-Time Typical | One-Time High | Notes |
|------|------------:|------------------:|--------------:|-------|
| Contract review retainer (govcon attorney) | $1,500 | $5,000 | $20,000 | For reviewing FAR clauses on first few bids. Can be hourly ($250-500/hr). |
| Records retention SOP (if outsourced) | $0 | $1,000 | $5,000 | Can DIY with FAR 4.7 template. Pay only if you want attorney-reviewed version. |
| Capability statement design | $0 | $500 | $2,000 | Can DIY. Professional design helps but isn't required. |

### Things Newport Does NOT Need (filtered out)

| Item | Why Not | Potential Cost Avoided |
|------|---------|----------------------:|
| SF 1408 / DCAA accounting system | Newport sells commercial products at catalog prices (firm-fixed-price). Cost-type work is not the play. | $15,000-$75,000 |
| CMMC / NIST 800-171 cybersecurity | Newport sells candy and dry goods, not defense supplies with CUI. | $104,000+ over 3 years |
| CAS (Cost Accounting Standards) | Exempt for small businesses and commercial items. | $10,000-$50,000 |
| GSA MAS Schedule (year one) | Premature. Build past performance on open-market bids first. Revisit in year 2-3. | $25,000-$75,000 onboarding |
| Single Audit / A-133 | Doesn't apply to procurement contractors selling goods. Only for grant recipients. | $15,000-$50,000 |
| Bonding (bid/performance/payment) | For construction contracts. Newport is wholesale supply. | 0.5%-4% of bonded amount |

## SECTION 5: First 6-Month Action Timeline

### Month 1-2: Foundation (cost: ~$0 + time)
- [ ] SAM.gov registration + UEI — set renewal calendar
- [ ] NAICS finalized (424410 primary) + rationale memo
- [ ] Records retention SOP drafted (FAR 4.7)
- [ ] Insurance reviewed — confirm GL/product liability meets municipal COI requirements
- [ ] Begin Apollo + Instantly setup (starts tech stack costs)

### Month 3-4: Florida + Local Activation (cost: ~$0 fees)
- [ ] Florida Substitute W-9 submitted + verified
- [ ] MFMP/VBS registration complete
- [ ] City of Plantation vendor application submitted
- [ ] Broward BPRO registration complete
- [ ] Sending domains purchased + mailboxes warming up

### Month 5-6: Bid Readiness (cost: $1,500-$5,000 attorney)
- [ ] Capability statement finalized
- [ ] Bid/no-bid rubric created
- [ ] Pricing approval process documented
- [ ] FAR 52.204-21 safeguarding controls documented
- [ ] First low-complexity bids submitted
- [ ] Post-bid lessons captured
- [ ] AI SDR campaigns live (lead gen → outreach pipeline running)

### Month 7-12: Scale
- [ ] SBA certifications explored (if eligible — WOSB, HUBZone, VetCert, 8(a))
- [ ] APEX Accelerator engaged (free coaching + bid reviews)
- [ ] Decide on GSA MAS path (year 2 decision)
- [ ] Year-end compliance review + SAM renewal
