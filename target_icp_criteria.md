# ICP TARGETING EXPANSION – ENTERPRISE ONLY
## Optimized for Maximum Accurate Capture (Apollo + Scraper)

Geography Constraint:
- United States
- United Kingdom
- European Union
- EEA
- Switzerland
- Norway
- Hungary
- Exclude Russia and Belarus

Only target enterprise-scale companies that meet volume requirements.

---

# SEGMENT A — BUYERS (ENTERPRISE GROCERY / RETAIL CHAINS)

## Qualification Threshold (MUST MEET AT LEAST ONE)

- 200+ employees
- $50M+ annual revenue
- 20+ retail locations
- Multi-state or multi-country footprint
- Centralized corporate procurement

Discard companies that do not meet these thresholds.

---

## Apollo Filters

### Company Size
- 200–10,000 employees
- Revenue $50M+

### Departments
- Purchasing
- Procurement
- Merchandising
- Supply Chain
- Strategy
- Commercial (Europe)

### Seniority
- Director
- VP
- CXO
- Head
- Partner
- Manager ONLY if company > 1,000 employees

# SCRAPER LOGIC RULES

Only capture leads if:
- Company meets enterprise size threshold
- Title matches inclusion keywords
- Title does not match exclusion keywords
- Company operates in US or approved Europe geography (including Hungary)

Deduplicate by:
- Company domain
- LinkedIn company URL
- Email

Discard all SMB or non-enterprise operators.
