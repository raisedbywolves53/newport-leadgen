# ICP TARGETING EXPANSION – ENTERPRISE ONLY
## Optimized for Maximum Accurate Capture (Apollo + Scraper)

Geography Constraint (per-segment):
- Segments A & B: United States, United Kingdom, EU/EEA/Switzerland
- Segment C: United States only
- Segment D: United States only
- Segment E: United States, Mexico, Central America, South America, Caribbean
- Exclude Russia and Belarus (global)

Only target companies that meet segment-specific size/volume requirements.

---

# SEGMENT A — BUYERS (ENTERPRISE GROCERY / RETAIL CHAINS)

## Qualification Threshold (MUST MEET AT LEAST ONE)

- 200+ employees
- $50M+ annual revenue
- 20+ retail locations
- Multi-state or multi-country footprint
- Centralized corporate procurement

Discard companies that do not meet these thresholds.

---

## Apollo Filters

### Company Size
- 200–10,000 employees
- Revenue $50M+

### Seniority

# SCRAPER LOGIC RULES

Only capture leads if:
- Company meets segment-specific size threshold
- Title matches inclusion keywords
- Title does not match exclusion keywords
- Company operates in segment's allowed geography

Deduplicate by:
- Company domain
- LinkedIn company URL
- Email

Discard all SMB or non-enterprise operators.
