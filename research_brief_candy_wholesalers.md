### 4D. TYPICAL COMPANY STRUCTURES

**Large National Distributors (McLane, Core-Mark, Nassau Candy):**
- 1,000-8,000+ employees
- C-suite (CEO, CFO, COO, CCO, CRO)
- VP-level leaders for each major function
- Regional and divisional structures
- Dedicated category buyers for candy/confectionery
- Centralized procurement with regional execution

**Mid-Size Specialty Distributors (Redstone Foods, BA Sweetie, Casani):**
- 50-500 employees
- Owner/President/GM at top
- 1-3 buyers covering candy categories
- Sales Manager or Director managing field teams
- Operations Manager overseeing warehouse/logistics
- Purchasing decisions often made by owner or 1-2 key buyers

**Small/Regional Distributors (McKeesport Candy, Wholesale Candy Shop, Arcade Snacks):**
- 10-50 employees
- Owner-operator model
- Owner often IS the buyer
- Small sales team (1-5 people)
- Key relationships are personal and trust-based

**Latin American Manufacturers/Distributors (Arcor, Colombina, Nutresa):**
- 5,000-20,000 employees
- Multi-country corporate structures
- Country managers with P&L responsibility
- Regional commercial directors
- Category managers for confectionery segments
- Export/international trade departments

## 8. NEWPORT STRATEGIC SCENARIOS

### SCENARIO 1: Newport SELLS TO Candy Distributors (as a supplier)

**Target Companies:** All distributors in Sections 7A-7D
**Target Titles:** Purchasing Manager, Buyer, Category Manager, Procurement Director, VP of Purchasing, VP of Merchandising
**Value Proposition:** Newport as a reliable wholesale source of candy/confectionery products at competitive pricing
**Key Entry Points:**
- NCA membership and Sweets & Snacks Expo attendance
- CDA membership for convenience distributor access
- Direct outreach to buyers at mid-size specialty distributors (Redstone, Nassau, BA Sweetie) who are always looking for new product sources
- Latin American distributors seeking US-sourced candy products

**Apollo/Scraper Targeting Notes:**
- NAICS 424450 as primary filter
- Title keywords: purchasing, procurement, buyer, category, merchandising, sourcing
- Company keywords: candy distributor, confectionery wholesale, candy wholesale
- Company size: 50+ employees for meaningful accounts
- Geography: US primarily, Latin America for export opportunities

### SCENARIO 2: Newport PARTNERS WITH Candy Distributors (co-distribution)

**Target Companies:** Regional distributors (7B), Latin American distributors (7C, 7D), cross-border traders (7E)
**Target Titles:** VP of Sales, VP of Business Development, Director of Sales, National Account Manager, Director of Channel Sales, Export Manager, Commercial Director
**Value Proposition:** Newport's distribution infrastructure complementing theirs; geographic coverage expansion; shared logistics for efficiency
**Key Entry Points:**
- Trade show networking (Sweets & Snacks Expo, ISM, Expo ANTAD)
- Outreach to complementary regional distributors (e.g., Newport covers regions they don't)
- Latin American companies wanting US distribution partnerships
- US ethnic candy distributors wanting broader mainstream distribution

**Apollo/Scraper Targeting Notes:**
- Title keywords: sales, business development, commercial, partnership, channel, wholesale, export, national account, key account
- Look for companies with complementary geographic coverage
- Mid-size companies (100-2,000 employees) most likely to be open to partnerships

### SCENARIO 3: Newport COMPETES WITH Candy Distributors (targeting their customers)

**Target Companies:** Retail customers OF the distributors listed above
**Target Titles:** Store Buyer, Category Manager, Purchasing Manager at RETAILERS (not the distributors themselves)
**Value Proposition:** Better pricing, service, or selection than incumbent candy distributors
**Key Entry Points:**
- Identify the customer bases of regional distributors
- Target convenience stores, grocery chains, gift shops, candy stores, dollar stores, pharmacies
- Focus on underserved regions or segments where current distributors have weak coverage
- Offer competitive pricing on high-velocity candy SKUs

**Apollo/Scraper Targeting Notes:**
- Target retail companies rather than distributor companies
- NAICS codes for retail: 445110 (Supermarkets), 445120 (Convenience Stores), 452210 (Department Stores), 453998 (Miscellaneous Retail)
- Title keywords: purchasing, buyer, category, merchandising at retail chains
- This scenario uses the existing Segment A (Buyers) framework from the ICP targeting criteria
